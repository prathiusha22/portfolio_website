Education

I completed my 10th standard at Shri Gurumuki Vidhyassharam, a CBSE school in Dindigul, where I secured an 80% score. However, for my higher secondary education, due to financial constraints, I moved to St. Joseph Higher Secondary School, a matriculation school in Dindigul, where I achieved an 83% score.

Upon completing my higher secondary education in 2022, I made the decision to forgo pursuing a traditional degree. Instead, my brother, who was a former student at Freshworks, strongly recommended me to join in Freshworks.

My educational journey has been shaped by my passion for coding and the software industry. While I chose not to pursue a traditional degree, I did receive a foundation in computer science and related subjects during my schooling years.

Throughout my schooling, I had the opportunity to explore various aspects of computer science, including the basics of programming. I found these subjects to be particularly fascinating, which motivated me to further delve into coding and software development.

Despite not pursuing a formal degree, I am committed to lifelong learning. I continue to educate myself through online courses, and self-learnt projects. This self-directed learning approach has allowed me to stay up-to-date with the latest developments in the software field and to hone my programming skills.

My schooling provided me with a solid foundation in computer science, and I have since taken it upon myself to deepen my knowledge and expertise through self-guided learning and practical projects in the software industry.
